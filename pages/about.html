About The Developer
Hi, my name is Brooke :) along with studying computer science at UCSC, I hold a strong and dear passion for guitar and music. I have both played and engulfed myself in the world of guitar for over 8 years now. Having been a teacher as well as a learner (being self taught myself) I've frustratingly experienced a chronic lack of comprehensive, accessible, and non-gimmicky ways of tracking learning progression, as well as centralizing foundational and commonly utilized features for learning and maintaining guitar skill ...
About GuitarBase
When it comes to tracking progress and learning songs, I've resorted to using spreedsheets and note taking apps for years; link after link of websites with specific, singular tools; yearning for a centralized place - designed specifically for guitar - that could aid me in staying organized and inspired as I continue to learn. This is what I set out to remedy with GuitarBase.
The Goal
This project is very much still in it's early stages, however I plan on building ontop of this project throughout the next couple of years as I deepen my full stack development skills, as I can then implement much more intricate and larger features. My personal goal would be to develop GuitarBase to the level that I would be able to rely on it fully, not needing to supplement my learning with any other platform or website.
